Banana Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/4 cups (156g) All Purpose Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cup (300ml) Soy Milk (or other non-dairy milk)
  • 2 Ripe Bananas (~200g)
  • 1 Tbsp Coconut Oil

Method:

  1. Put your waffle iron on to begin heating up.
  2. Sift your flour into a mixing bowl and add the baking powder and salt.
  3. Add the bananas and soy milk (or other non-dairy milk) to a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Pour this blended mix into the mixing bowl. Add the coconut oil and vanilla extract and using a hand whisk quickly whisk it to remove any lumps.
  5. When your waffle iron is hot enough, pour out enough batter (usually around 1/3 cup per waffle) to cover the waffle iron on each side.
  6. Bake for around 8 minutes or until very golden brown and crispy.
